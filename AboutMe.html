I'm Tiffany Clements, born on the 11th of October 1996. I began my teaching career at the age of 18, finishing my HSC as I completed my certificate three in Early Childhood Education and Care. I continued with this career for seven years, studying my Bachelor of Early Childhood. In the mist of the Coivd-19 Outbreak, I felt like I could be doing more, feeling lost and unsure, I started a six month course to stimulate my brain and see if i was capable of more. Guided by my passion for education, I began my double degree, Bachelor of Education (Secondary) / Bachelor of Arts (Technology). The four year journey began in 2021, currently in my third year, embarking on fulfilling my path to nurturing and stimulating our future generation.

Welcome to My Teaching Portfolio
